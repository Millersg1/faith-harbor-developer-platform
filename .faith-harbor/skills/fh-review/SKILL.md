# Faith Harbor Review

Use before launch, publication, deployment, client delivery, or declaring work complete.

## Review Sequence

1. Requested outcome
2. Accuracy and unsupported claims
3. Brand consistency
4. Content completeness
5. Accessibility
6. Responsive behavior
7. Functionality
8. Security and privacy
9. Performance
10. SEO or metadata
11. Testing evidence
12. Backup and rollback
13. Known limitations

## Rules

- Do not approve work that still contains placeholders.
- Do not claim tests passed without evidence.
- Do not approve unsupported marketing claims.
- Do not mark publishing stages complete prematurely.
- Document remaining risks clearly.
