# Faith Harbor OS Product Roadmap

**Version:** 1.0  
**Author:** Pastor Shawn Miller  
**Status:** Active

> **Governance:** This document shall be interpreted and maintained in accordance with the Faith Harbor Engineering Handbook.

---

## Vision

Faith Harbor OS is the intelligent operating system powering every Faith Harbor
LLC business.

The platform will provide AI orchestration, business automation, ministry tools,
publishing support, hosting management, and intelligent workflows from a single
unified system.

---

## Guiding Principles

Every release should improve one or more of the following:

- Simplicity
- Reliability
- Performance
- Automation
- Customer Value
