# Faith Harbor OS

> The Intelligent Business Operating System for Faith Harbor LLC

Faith Harbor OS is a modular AI-powered platform designed to support every Faith Harbor business through intelligent automation, provider-agnostic AI integration, workflow orchestration, and business management tools.

---

## Vision

Faith Harbor OS serves as the technology foundation for:

- Faith Harbor Web Solutions
- Faith Harbor Web Hosting
- Faith Harbor Ministry Platform
- Faith Harbor Publishing
- Faith Harbor Grief Support
- Future Faith Harbor businesses

Our goal is one unified operating system that powers every aspect of the organization.
